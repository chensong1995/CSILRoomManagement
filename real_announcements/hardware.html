About special GPUs in the lab

We have installed 15 new video cards (NVidia Geforce GTX 1050 Ti, from ZOTAC) in ASB 9840. The computers equiped with these cards are: asb9840-a02 to a08 and asb9840-b##. These cards have the CUDA Compute Capability of 6.1. The CUDA development tools are configured under Ubuntu on these systems.

There are 9 descrete video cards (NVidia Geforce GT 720, MSI N720-2GD3LP) in ASB 9838 North-East side. The computers equiped with these cards are: asb9838n-a12 to a16 and asb9838n-b13 to b16. These cards have the CUDA Compute Capability of 3.5. The CUDA development tools (incl. Caffe) are configured under Ubuntu on these systems. (Please note: to utilize the projector in the area, computer asb9838n-a12 is now located at the west side of the hallway.)

People taking paralle computing or graphics related courses have priority to use these systems.

About Projectors in the lab

There is a mounted projector for each study area; each is accompanied by a remote, a motorized screen and a special VGA cable. You may locate the projector's VGA connector near these workstations:

  • asb9700-a05
  • asb9804-a05
  • asb9838n-a07 (displays to two projectors)
  • asb9820-a01
  • asb9840-a05

Please remember, after your presentation is finished:

Turn off the projector to save the lamp and electricity.
Put the screen back to the top.
Make sure the switch (for screen motors) is on "off" (not up, not down).
detach the VGA cable from the computer.

Click here for more details on how to use the projectors.

About powerbars in the lab

The powerbars are provided for personal computing devices only. Plugging in any other devices is prohibited.

Please do not unplug any device already plugged into the powerbar - others may be using the device.

Please remember to unplug & bag your own device before you leave.

Safety warning: trip hazard -  you may only use the powerbar on the same row you sit on; otherwise, the cables may cause people stumble.

top

About DisplayPort cables and mini DisplayPort cables in the lab

All DisplayPort cables and mini DisplayPort cables are for the School's equipment use only.

Warning: disconnecting any DisplayPort cable or mini DisplayPort cable from the CSIL workstations for personal use is prohibited.

The CSIL access will be revoked if you use any of these cables for your personal device.

top

About extra VGA cables in the lab

The extra VGA cables are provided for people to hook up our LCD panels to their personal laptops.

If you change the display settings of the monitor to use the extra VGA connection as the input source, please reset to proper one when you leave - this means you should see the login screen on the LCD panel.

Warning: disconnecting any VGA cable from the CSIL workstations for personal use is prohibited.

Special notes on programming

Operating System (O/S) level programming (e.g. with fork() calls) must be done on Linux workstations in CSIL. No other hosts should be used on SFU network, no exceptions.
